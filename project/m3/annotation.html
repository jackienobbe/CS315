Usage of JS

  • For this milestone, JS was primarily used to view the list of all bands and shows available.
    • Currently, a static list of bands and shows are displayed on their respective pages (dispBands.phpdispShows.php)
    • Each band or show name is clickable, and the details will appear for that specific band or show. There is also an early 'delete' function that just removes the band or show from the page when clicked, but eventually will remove the item from the database.
    • In this milestone, a search/filter function was created to search through the items listed on the bands and shows page. (The glitch with this function is that in order to get items to show once they've disappeared is to erase the whole query.)
